Specialty Pharmacy Services

Q: What are “Specialty Pharmacies”?

A: Specialty pharmacy is a branch of pharmacy that emphasizes on delivering medicines for patients with complex, chronic, or rare medical ailments requiring specialized care. These medicines are typically high-cost, high-touch drugs necessitating special handling, storage, and administration standards. The specialty pharmacies generally employ trained pharmacists, nurses, and other medical professionals to closely coordinate with patients, their doctors, and insurance providers to synchronize care and amplify patient results. Services offered encompass medication counseling, therapy management, assistance in insurance and financial areas, and home delivery services.

Q: What are “Specialty Pharmacies”?

A: Specialty pharmacies focus on providing medicines for patients with complex or rare conditions. These high-cost drugs require specific care in handling, storage, and administration. Specialty pharmacies employ experts to collaborate closely with patients, doctors, and insurers, offering services like medication counseling, therapy management, insurance assistance, and home deliveries.
